Who We Are

Stone Hub
Traders

For over seven decades, Stone Hub Traders has stood at the forefront of premium stone craftsmanship — rooted in Makrana, Rajasthan.

Makrana marble is not simply a material — it is the stone that built the Taj Mahal. Our family has worked this material across three generations, developing an intimacy with its character, its grain, and its limits.

From custom home mandirs that become a family's most cherished possession, to intricate jaali screens and architectural flooring for commercial and sacred spaces — every commission is approached with the same discipline: understand the space, respect the stone, deliver something lasting.

We serve clients across India and internationally — Dubai, USA, UK, Australia, and Tanzania — carrying a heritage built on craft, not volume.
