  • Polished marble has a high-gloss mirror-like finish that brings out the depth of natural veining — standard for mandirs, tabletops, and decorative pieces. Honed marble is ground to a smooth, matte surface that feels more tactile and subdued — preferred for wall cladding and contemporary minimalist interiors. Both finishes are available across our full range.
  • Clean marble with a soft cloth and a pH-neutral cleaner. Avoid acidic products such as vinegar, lemon juice, or bleach. Mandirs and decorative pieces need only regular dusting.
